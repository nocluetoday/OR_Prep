# Urology Curriculum Map

Urology is the first example curriculum. These modules should use the same general module structure as any other specialty.

## Oncology

- Prostate cancer
- Bladder cancer
- Kidney cancer
- Testicular cancer
- Penile cancer

## Benign and Functional Urology

- BPH / lower urinary tract symptoms
- Overactive bladder
- Urinary incontinence
- Neurogenic bladder

## Stones and Endourology

- Nephrolithiasis
- Ureteral stones
- Metabolic stone evaluation
- Stone prevention

## Infection and Inflammation

- Urinary tract infection
- Prostatitis
- Epididymitis / orchitis
- Fournier gangrene and urologic emergencies

## Andrology and Reproductive Urology

- Erectile dysfunction
- Male infertility
- Peyronie disease
- Testosterone deficiency

## Pediatrics

- Cryptorchidism
- Vesicoureteral reflux
- Hypospadias
- Pediatric hydronephrosis

## Ethics, Communication, and Professionalism

- Informed consent
- Shared decision-making
- Disclosure of complications
- Resource allocation
- Professional boundaries
- End-of-life conversations in urologic oncology
